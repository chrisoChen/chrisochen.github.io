Things to Know Before You Place Your First Order

September 3, 2020

moovez is a new addition to your daily life. Chances are, this is a new way of moving your things, but have no fear – it is a much easier process than you may expect! So, you might be wondering how moovez works? Well, to answer some of those questions, here is a list of questions you may have!

What is moovez?

moovez, as a company, is committed to helping people transport their things easier than ever before. Instead of calling a friend, renting a truck, or carrying it yourself, moovez is the place to call for when you need help. Whether it is a large cabinet going to a new apartment or a pot of flowers for your grandmother, simply order a driver through the moovez app, and we will make sure the item gets from point A to point B in the quickest time possible. We currently operate in Alberta only, but keep an eye out in the future for further expansion!

How Long Will It Take for A Delivery to Be Completed?

moovez operates through an instant order response system, meaning drivers will be on the road and ready to complete your delivery the second you place it. No more waiting in line at a rental shop, as after a few taps on our app, a delivery driver will be en route to help you out! The delivery time will depend on the distance between pickup and drop-off locations, but our drivers are committed to delivering your items as fast as possible!

Am I Allowed to Be in The Delivery Vehicle with My Things?

Unfortunately, due to our safety policies, you cannot ride in the vehicle with your things. While similar, we are not a ride-sharing app, and can only perform deliveries of objects, not people. However, to put your mind at ease, we have a $??? Insurance policy with (insert insurance org.) to ensure that your things are in the safest of hands!

How Much Will A Delivery Cost?

There is no ‘set’ delivery fee for each order, as every order will be different. The fee is calculated based on number of items, distance between locations, size of vehicle, and how much labor will be required of our drivers. Smaller orders that travel a small distance will cost less than a large order that travels across the city, for an example.

Will There Be Ratings Like Other Apps?

Yes. Very similar to other apps that are of a similar nature to ours, there will be a rating system where the customer may rate the driver, and the driver may rate the customer. This will allow for us to monitor each experience for both our drivers and customers, and solve any potential issues that may come up.

Are There Any Restrictions to What Can Be Delivered?

While there are no specific exclusions to what can be delivered, it is recommended that extremely valuable or fragile items are not delivered using this platform. Due to the busy nature of our drivers, it can be difficult to heed extreme caution every second of every order, and due to the fast-paced nature, we suggest that your most cherished and expensive items are transported utilizing a different method.

How Will I Know Which Vehicle to Order?

When placing an order through our website, you will have the option of seeing the recommendations for certain items, as well as the measurements of our vehicles. For some items, the correct vehicle choice will be clear, but for the items that could be delivered by more than one, we are happy to give guidance on which will be the best vehicle for your delivery!
