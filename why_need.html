Why Would I Need moovez?

September 3, 2020

A question you might have is why would I need someone else to move my things? The simple answer is, life gets in the way sometimes. Whether it’s a lack of time, not a big enough car, or any other reason, we are here to help you move your things quickly and efficiently, because life gets in the way!

Imagine this: you have a big move planned for a weekend, and have to have everything moved by Sunday night. On Friday, a game of softball goes awry, and you throw out your back, leaving you in pain and unable to lift things. Now, usually, this would be a very tough scenario to be a part of, given the urgency of the move, but the inability to complete it. Well, these issues are not a worry anymore. With a couple of taps on your mobile device or computer, we can have a driver arrive at location ‘A’, pack up your things, deliver them to location ‘B’, and drop them off. We are made to be a solution when life gets in the way! Don’t let life get in the way of moving into your dream home!

Except when you go to pick up the desk, it is way bigger than you expected, and will definitely not fit in the back of your car. This desk is perfect, but it doesn’t fit? What ever will you do!?! Simple. Order a cargo van off of our app, load the desk in, and meet the driver back at your home. Don’t let life get in the way of the office of your dreams!
